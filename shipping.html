Shipping Policy

Delivery Coverage

Seedora currently delivers across serviceable Indian pincodes, with priority service for Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

Most orders are packed fresh and dispatched within 24 to 48 business hours after order confirmation.

Delivery Charges

Delivery charge is calculated by pincode and order value during checkout. Andhra Pradesh and Telangana receive the fastest and lowest delivery slab.

Estimated Delivery

Local AP/Telangana orders usually arrive in 1 to 3 business days. Other serviceable Indian locations may take 2 to 7 business days.
